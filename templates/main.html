Introduction

The campus of National Chengchi University is located at the foot of the mountain hills, with abundance of wild or stray animal presence. In case of danger or incidents where human intervention was needed, students are prone to share information on social media. But due to the general lack of management and the use of applications that weren't designed for this specific purpose, the spread of information can often be quite inefficient. To solve this problem, we've built this very site, in hope to better the aforementioned situation.
